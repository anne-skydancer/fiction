# Anne's Sub — Novella Outline and Bible

## Overview

**Title:** Anne's Sub
**Genre:** Adult romance / BDSM / Women's fiction
**Setting:** Napa Valley, California (Yountville, St. Helena, Vallejo)
**Timeline:** Approximately 4-5 months from first meeting to end
**POV:** Third person limited, alternating between Anne and Amy

**Core Theme:** Two wounded people finding healing through D/s — Anne recovering from grief, Amy discovering her identity. The collar as symbol of freedom, not constraint.

---

## Three-Part Structure

### Part One: The Meeting (Chapters 1-3)
Chance encounter → connection → consideration offered

### Part Two: Consideration (Chapters 4-7)
Training → Julia's shadow → Amy's family arrives → the crisis → resolution

### Part Three: The Collar (Chapters 8-11)
Ceremony → consummation → new beginning
